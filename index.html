How we use Klaviyo

Customer Segmentation

We create dynamic customer segments that update in real time - so that you can spend your time on what counts, instead of manually managing lists.

Site Activity Tracking

We track how customers behave on your website and use that information to create segments. This lets us design highly personalized emails that net you massive amounts of sales.

Email Flow Automation

We create automated email flows that target based on special events and conditions, like a purchase or subscribing to a list. That way, you know your email will always show up in the right place at the right time.

Targeted Marketing
